What we do

From hand painted murals and floors to lettering designed book covers, we love all things art and experimental design. Fortunate to have been able to collaborate with a series of amazing clients, our work continues to grow as we focus on exploring new aesthetics and innovating in our line of work.

Mural Art

We’ve had the opportunity to create murals with a variety of styles, ranging from bold super-graphics, illustrative lettering and different kinds of illustration. You can find our work in locations such as Köln, Berlin, Hamburg, Dublin, Toronto, Chicago and Denver.

Experimental Type

Calligraphy, illustrative lettering, experimental typography, you name it: we love it! We believe letters go beyond the words they were meant to form, and they invite us to play with shapes & colors and turn them into something more. Amongst our type work you will find book covers, posters, murals, floor decorations, postcards and more.

Illustration

Our illustrative work can be found in software applications, books, educational material, posters, postcards and even as part of a clothing brand campaign. Want to try out something different? Just let us know!
